The Problem
Every day, service-based businesses like resorts, hotels, luxury clubs, local salons, and startups face a massive operational challenge. Hiring a full-time, human receptionist is incredibly expensive once you factor in base salaries, taxes, healthcare, and vacation days, consuming a massive portion of an owner's overhead budget. Even worse, humans can only work set hours, handle one call at a time, take sick leave, and experience off-days where customer service slips. On the other end of the spectrum, smaller local startups and boutique salons cannot afford a receptionist at all. Because owners are busy running day-to-day operations—cutting hair, managing guests, or tracking inventory—the phone sits there ringing. When a call goes unanswered, that customer immediately hangs up and calls a competitor, causing these businesses to constantly lose valuable bookings and revenue simply because they cannot be by the phone every single second.

The Solution & Core Features
This is exactly why we built Harkly AI (available live at harklyai.up.railway.app). Harkly is an intelligent, agentic voice SaaS that acts as a flawless, 24/7 virtual receptionist for any business. Unlike old-school robotic phone menus that frustrate callers, Harkly uses ultra-fast, sub-500ms natural language processing so it listens, understands, and speaks just like a real person. Business owners can use our simple drag-and-drop dashboard to configure their agent in minutes without writing a single line of code. It comes fully equipped with multi-language support, allowing it to seamlessly switch between English, Hindi, Spanish, and more to serve a diverse customer base. You can choose custom voices that perfectly match your brand's tone, leveraging ElevenLabs for lifelike, human voice quality, GPT/Gemini for conversational intellect, and Vapi for smooth call orchestration. Crucially, Harkly has agentic abilities, meaning it can use integrated apps like Google Calendar to set appointments, WhatsApp to send concise call transcripts or emergency notifications, and Gmail to dispatch confirmation messages, all while the AI is still active on the live call.

Uniqueness & The Competition
What makes Harkly completely revolutionary is how it departs from standard industry competition. Most existing tools in this space are either basic text chatbots or rigid, frustrating IVR answering systems that feel cold and mechanical to the customer. Harkly, on the other hand, provides a completely fluid, human-like conversation. Our uniqueness lies in our agentic behavior; the system doesn't just follow a static script, but instead dynamically understands customer intent, handles interruptions gracefully, and solves complex scheduling tasks on the fly. The entire workflow is seamless: a user connects their verified phone number, and when someone rings it, the agent activates with a custom greeting, instantly analyzes requests using intelligent APIs, and simultaneously manages verified integrated apps based on the business info provided. While big enterprise call-center software exists, it requires months of expensive setup and dedicated IT teams. Harkly gives small salons and mid-sized resorts the exact same world-class AI capability instantly through a beautiful, accessible dashboard that anyone can set up over a coffee break.
